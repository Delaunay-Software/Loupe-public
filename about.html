The Game

Loupe is an image guessing game. Each challenge presents you with a scrambled, obfuscated image. Your goal is to figure out what the image shows before your score runs out. Tap tiles to reveal more of the image, or type your guess at any time.

How It Works

Daily Challenges

A new image every day, drawn from our curated image packs. Play the daily challenge to build your streak and climb the leaderboard.

Packs

Collections of themed images you can play at your own pace. Each pack has three difficulty levels. Complete a level to unlock the quiz.

Quizzes

After completing a pack level, test your knowledge of the interesting facts you've learned. 5 rounds, 10 questions each, 10 seconds per question.

Tournaments

Compete against other players for prizes. Tournament packs feature quiz competitions where the best score wins a free pack.

Scoring

Your score starts at the maximum and decreases as you reveal more of the image or make wrong guesses. Answer correctly for full points, or give a partial answer for 40% of the score. Speed matters — a time bonus rewards fast answers.
