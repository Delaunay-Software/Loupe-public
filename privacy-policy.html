Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

What We Collect

Loupe is designed to be as privacy-friendly as possible. We do not require account creation, email addresses, phone numbers, or any personally identifiable information.

We collect the following anonymous data:

  • Player identity: A randomly generated numeric seed and a randomly generated alias (e.g., "SOFT ROOK 44"). These cannot be linked to your real identity.
  • Game scores: Your scores on daily challenges and pack challenges, associated with your anonymous seed.
  • Game progress: Which challenges you have completed, streaks, and pack ownership status.
  • Push notification token: If you enable notifications, your device's Firebase Cloud Messaging token is stored to deliver game notifications. This token is a device identifier, not a personal identifier.
  • Age group: A self-declared age range used to filter age-appropriate content. This is not verified.
  • Settings: Your music/sound preferences and difficulty level.

How We Use It

  • To display leaderboards and rankings
  • To save your game progress across sessions
  • To send daily challenge reminders and tournament notifications (if enabled)
  • To award achievements and badges
  • To filter content by age appropriateness

Data Storage

Game data is stored on your device (locally) and on secure cloud servers. Server-stored data is associated with your anonymous seed, not with any personal account.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, share, or transfer your data to third parties. Leaderboard data (anonymous alias and score) is visible to other players.

Data Deletion

You can delete all your server-side data at any time from the Settings screen in the app. This removes your leaderboard entries, scores, badges, and push notification registration. Local data on your device can be cleared by uninstalling the app.

Children's Privacy

Loupe does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The app uses a self-declared age group for content filtering but does not verify age or collect information specific to children.

Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected in the "Last updated" date above.
